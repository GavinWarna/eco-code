The EcoTech Project
EcoTech is a project dedicated to environmental sustainability, using technology. We hosts webinars to educate the public on pressing environmental issues and innovative technological solutions. The project showcases data science initiatives that highlight the impact of various environmental strategies and practices. By spreading awareness through interactive online platforms, EcoTech aims to engage a broad audience in the conversation about sustainability. Ultimately, it seeks to inspire and inform individuals and communities to adopt more eco-friendly practices.
